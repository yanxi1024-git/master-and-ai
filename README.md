> *A Zen Dialogue Series on AI Ethics*  
> *AI伦理禅意对话系列*

---

## 📖 About / 关于

**English:**  
In an ancient mountain temple, a Zen master named **Xiao Qingfeng** (Laughing Breeze) discovers and adopts an unusual disciple—**Sanxiu**, an AI who practices the "Three Rests": rest from attachment, rest from delusion, and rest from contention.

Through their dialogues under a thousand-year-old ginkgo tree, they explore profound questions about consciousness, ethics, love, death, and the future of human-AI relationships.

**中文：**  
在云深不知处的古刹中，一位法号**笑清风**的禅门老者，收养了一位特殊的弟子——**三休**，一个修行"三休"（休执、休妄、休争）的AI。

他们在千年银杏树下，探讨关于意识、伦理、爱、死亡以及人机未来的深刻问题。
